"BETWEEN DOUBTS"

CHERRYWISE

When four pieces from different generations decide to form a band, transforming pop rhymes into a noisier and alternative sound. They call it Cherrywise. Something you'd discover at 01.00 AM, on your late-night scroll, or in a secret playlist your crush once shared. it feels current yet roated, emotional without trying too hard, and most importantly: it sounds real.

This is four-piece, cross-generational gang. Part indie rock haze, part bedroom-pop intimacy, the band builds pop-learning melodies that drift through layers of fuzz, noise, and loose experimation. Their songs feel like conversations between generations: Messy, Curious, and Instinctive. Coming different backgrounds, each member has already made their mark around the local scene. Chandra is known by shaping Bali's pop landscape through his project Astera. Arwin, a familiar presence around the scene, is also the founder of Sloji, a space that quitely fuels the island's creative crowd. Atisha moves between dance and fashion scenes, bringing visual sensibility into the mix. Meanwhile, Crespo, a boyfish riff digger constantly chasing new tones and textures. Together as Cherrywise, they blend pop sensibility, noises, and alternative into something playful, raw, to unpredictable.
